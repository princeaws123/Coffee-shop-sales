This project focuses on analyzing coffee shop sales data. The goal was to clean, process, and visualize sales data to extract meaningful insights that can help improve business decision-making.

An interactive dashboard was created using Microsoft Excel to present key metrics such as sales trends, product performance, and customer behavior.


Objectives...
Analyze overall sales performance
Identify top-selling products
Track sales trends over time
Understand customer purchasing patterns
Provide actionable business insights

Tools & Technologies Used...

Pivot Tables
Pivot Charts
Data Cleaning
Data Validation
Dashboard Design

Key Insights...

Certain products contribute the majority of revenue (top performers)
Peak sales occur during specific hours of the day
Seasonal or monthly trends affect sales patterns
Some categories consistently outperform others

Business Recommendations...

Focus on promoting high-performing products
Optimize staffing during peak hours
Introduce offers during low-sales periods
Expand popular product categories
